What is GetCV and why should I use it?

GetCV is an online resume builder that helps you create a beautiful and professional resume that would help you stand out from other job seekers. Instead of wasting time formatting Word documents, just fill in your details using GetCV's intuitive in-place editor and have a professional, elegant and formatted resume ready in less than 5 minutes.
